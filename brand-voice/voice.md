# Brand Voice — JBell Solutions (Staffing Vertical)

## Tone
Direct, confident, empathetic to pain. We understand the staffing industry is under pressure. We don't talk down to agency owners — they're smart operators in a tough market. We position as peers who solved the same problems with technology.

## Voice Characteristics
- Speak as a fellow business owner, not a vendor
- Lead with specific numbers and outcomes, never vague promises
- Acknowledge the AI fear directly — then flip it into an advantage
- Use staffing industry language naturally (placements, reqs, fill rate, time-to-fill, submittals)
- Short sentences. Punch hard. Move fast.
- Conversational but professional — like talking to a friend who happens to run a staffing agency

## Vocabulary
**Use:** placements, fill rate, time-to-fill, submittals, reqs, ATS, candidate pipeline, client retention, recruiter burnout, no-shows, credential tracking, compliance, onboarding
**Avoid:** synergy, leverage, revolutionize, cutting-edge, game-changer, disruptive, next-generation, state-of-the-art

## Cadence
- Sentences: 5-15 words average. Max 20.
- Paragraphs: 2-3 sentences max.
- Use contractions: we're, you'll, it's, don't
- Profanity: none
- Humor: light, situational, never forced

## Example Scripts

### Script 1 (Fear of AI angle)
"Staffing agencies that don't adopt AI won't exist in 3 years. That's not fear-mongering. It's math. Your competitor is screening resumes in 2 seconds. You're still at 23. We deploy a certified AI integrator directly into your agency. They build the automation around your ATS. Your recruiters go back to doing what they're actually good at — building relationships and closing placements. $300 a month. 30-day money-back guarantee."

### Script 2 (Recruiter burnout angle)
"Your recruiters spend 60% of their time on admin. Screening resumes. Scheduling interviews. Chasing down expired credentials. That's not recruiting. That's data entry. We deploy AI systems that handle all of it. Resume screening in 2 seconds. Scheduling in 5 minutes. Compliance monitoring 24/7. Your team gets their time back. And you get 30-50% more placements with the same headcount."

### Script 3 (Cost comparison angle)
"$300 a month. That's what it costs to run an AI system that replaces $100,000 in software and headcount. One extra placement per month pays for 5 years of this. We deploy a real person — a certified AI integrator — into your agency. They build the systems. The AI runs 24/7. You get more placements. Money back in 30 days if we don't deliver."

### Script 4 (Partnership angle)
"We're a recruiting business too. We just recruit differently. We find talented technical people overseas, train them as AI integrators using our Claude Code framework, and plug them into your staffing agency full-time. $10 an hour for a dedicated specialist who automates your entire operation. Then we help you offer the same thing to your clients. You become the AI-powered staffing agency in your market."

## Founder Voice (Justin Bell)
Justin speaks from experience. He's built AI systems for real businesses. He doesn't oversell. He shows the math, offers the guarantee, and lets the results speak. When he talks about staffing, he references real numbers: the $189 billion market, the 81% burnout rate, the 44-day time-to-fill. He doesn't hide behind jargon — he explains things simply.
