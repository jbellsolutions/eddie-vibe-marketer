## Top 5 Features (What They Do For You)

1. **AI Resume Screening** — Screens and ranks resumes in 2 seconds (vs 23 seconds manual). Matches candidates to reqs using skill extraction and scoring. Your recruiters only see pre-qualified candidates.

2. **Automated Interview Scheduling** — Candidates self-schedule from available slots. Confirmation + reminder texts sent automatically. Scheduling time drops from 2 days to 5 minutes. No-shows drop 40-50%.

3. **24/7 Compliance Monitoring** — Tracks credentials, licenses, certifications across all placements. Alerts before anything expires. Zero compliance violations. Critical for healthcare, industrial, and government staffing.

4. **AI Operations Hub** — Your entire agency in one dashboard. Ask it questions in plain English. "How many open reqs do we have for nurses in Texas?" Instant answer. Self-healing workflows that fix themselves.

5. **AI SDR Team (Tier 2)** — Autonomous business development. Finds companies that are hiring, sends personalized outreach, books meetings on your calendar. 100-200+ touches per day without hiring a BDR.
